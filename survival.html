Digital Pathology · Survival Analysis

Methodology

Data

Pancreatic tumour tissue samples from patients who received chemotherapy before surgery, scored for protein expression by immunohistochemistry alongside clinical follow-up and survival data.

Modelling

Kaplan-Meier estimation with log-rank testing between high- and low-expression groups, a chi-square balance check against clinical variables, and univariable/multivariable Cox proportional hazards regression with a hazard-ratio forest plot. Subgroup analysis by treatment response and chemotherapy regimen for the neoadjuvant cohort.

Tools

Digital pathology software for IHC scoring, with statistical analysis in Python (lifelines, pandas), deployed as an interactive Streamlit app, aimed at a peer-reviewed publication.

Status

Ongoing — this page will be updated with figures and results as IHC scoring and survival analysis are completed.
