Methodology

Data

Pancreatic tumour tissue samples from patients who received chemotherapy before surgery, scored for protein expression by immunohistochemistry alongside clinical follow-up and survival data.

Modelling

Kaplan-Meier estimation to compare survival between high- and low-expression groups, and Cox proportional hazards regression to adjust for chemotherapy response, tumour stage, and age.

Tools

Digital pathology software for IHC scoring, with statistical analysis in Python, aimed at a peer-reviewed publication.
